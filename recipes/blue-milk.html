Blue Milk

Description

First appearing in the 1977 film Star Wars: A New Hope, blue milk was one of the beverages Luke Skywalker drank while growing up on the planet Tatooine.

Ingredients

  • 1-1/2 cups whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • blue food gel dye

Steps

  1. Stir together the milk, sugar, and vanilla.
  2. When the sugar is dissolved, stir in a drop of blue food gel dye until combined.
  3. Keep in the refrigerator until ready to drink.
