Green Milk

Description

In the 2017 film Star Wars: The Last Jedi, Luke Skywalker drank this beverage during his self-imposed exile on the planet Ahch-To.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla
  • 1/2 teaspoon almond extract
  • green food gel dye

Steps

  1. In a small saucepan over medium heat, bring the milk and sugar to a simmer. Stir to combine.
  2. When the sugar is dissolved, stir in the vanilla, almond extract, and drop of green food gel dye.
  3. Stir together, then pour into a bowl.
